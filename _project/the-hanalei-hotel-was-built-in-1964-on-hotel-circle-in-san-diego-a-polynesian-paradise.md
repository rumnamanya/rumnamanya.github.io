The Hanalei Hotel was built in 1964 on Hotel Circle in San Diego. A Polynesian paradise offering complete hotel services and exotic Polynesian restaurant and lounge. It was later stripped of it's Tiki charm in 1981 and constructed into the Crowne Plaza Hotel.
