In this small modern kitchen, wood countertops and backsplash compliment the warm brick while white cabinets, stainless steel appliances, and open shelving with built in lighting help keep the space feeling bright. The combination of the new materials and the old exposed brick creates a modern feel mixed with a hint of rustic charm.
