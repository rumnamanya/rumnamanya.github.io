STAR GATES: Choga Zanbil Ziggurat, PERSIA (NOW IRAN) It is one of the few existent ziggurats outside of Mesopotamia. Choga Zanbil, Ziggurat, Iran Chogha Zanbil (In Farsi language: چغازنبيل‎); Elamite: Dur Untash) is an ancient Elamite complex in the Khuzestan province of (now) IRAN. Chogha in Bakhtiari means hill. It lies approximately 42 km (26 mi) south-southwest of Dezfoul, 30 km (19 mi) west of Susa and 80 km (50 mi) north of Ahvaz.
