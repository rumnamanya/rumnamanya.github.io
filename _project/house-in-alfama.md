House in Alfama, Portugal by Matos Gameiro Arquitecto, is a renovation of  one of the few medieval structures in the area.  In the most dramatic moment a pool covers the entire courtyard area. This “room of water” gives reference to an ancient bathing culture rarely embraced in modern times.
