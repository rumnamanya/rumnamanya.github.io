Noria updates the traditional window air conditioner with a fresh, modern design and an app-based interface that works with iOS & Android devices. At just 6-inches tall, it is lightweight & easy to install. At 5,000 BTU it will quickly & efficiently cool a 10 X 16 room. It also features a fresh air mode that eliminates the compressor to simply circulate fresh air in from outside.
