Full Moon Japanese maple trees cascade and bloom in purplish red, making them a beautiful addition to any landscape. The Full Moon maple does well in full sun to partial shade, it requires medium water, and it is low maintenance. The foliage turns a wonderful yellow and red color in the fall.
