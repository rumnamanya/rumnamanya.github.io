The Sketch Design phase follows on after the Analysis phase.  After digesting the analysis information and considering the opportunities and constraints the designer is able to test their thoughts and ideas through illustration. Utilising hand graphics the trained professional is able to accurately assess scale and form and will set about integrating the programmed amenities and elements into the landscape in a logical and coherent manner.
