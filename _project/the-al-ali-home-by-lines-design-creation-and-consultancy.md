The Al Ali Home Kuwait City, Al Asimah Governate, Kuwait. Modern, spacious villa elegantly furnished to incorporate style and comfort in the space. The key feature of this villa is to incorporate an urban garden that is a part of the exterior and melds seamlessly with the internal garden. The garden is framed within the facade of the villa adding organic lines to the solid concrete and mesh structure. Designed by Lines Design Creation and Consultancy based in Kuwait.
